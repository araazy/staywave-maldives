Our Story

StayWave Maldives was created to make planning a Fuvahmulah dive trip feel easier, more transparent, and far more personal.

We know that a great dive holiday is never just about the dives. It is about staying somewhere comfortable, understanding the island, feeling safe in the water, and having every detail handled with care.

That is why we bring together diving insight, accommodation options, island knowledge, and responsive trip planning in one place. Our goal is simple: help every guest experience Fuvahmulah with confidence and unforgettable memories.

  • Local-first planningThoughtful recommendations shaped by island knowledge.
  • Diver-focused staysAccommodation options that work around your holiday style.
  • Clear communicationFast, personalised support from enquiry to arrival.

Why Fuvahmulah

A destination unlike anywhere else in the Maldives, known for its pelagic encounters, local character, and memorable island atmosphere.

Iconic Pelagic Encounters

Fuvahmulah is globally recognised for consistent shark and big-fish encounters that make every dive feel exciting and distinctive.

Authentic Island Life

Guests enjoy a real Maldivian island environment with welcoming local culture, independent cafés, and a stronger sense of place.

Adventure with Comfort

The island offers the rare balance of thrilling diving and accessible accommodation choices for different travel styles and budgets.
