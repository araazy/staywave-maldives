Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common accommodation questions from our guests.

Can I switch accommodation categories after booking? +

Yes, subject to availability. If you would like to upgrade or change your accommodation category after your holiday is confirmed, please contact us as soon as possible and we will do our best to accommodate the request.

Does my choice of accommodation affect the diving package? +

No. Your diving package — including the number of guided dives, dive sites, and all included services — remains exactly the same regardless of which accommodation category you choose. The accommodation is a separate component of your holiday.

Are meals included in the accommodation price? +

Basic and Standard packages are room-only. Premium Stay and Deluxe Stay guests can add a meal plan — Breakfast, Half Board, or Full Board — during the holiday planning process. See the Meal Plans section above for pricing.

What is the difference between a homestay and a guesthouse? +

A homestay is a private room in a local family home, offering an authentic Maldivian experience at a budget-friendly rate. A guesthouse is a dedicated accommodation property with more standardised facilities, daily housekeeping, and a slightly higher level of service.

Is the swimming pool at the Deluxe Stay suitable for use alongside diving? +

Yes. The pool is available for leisure use throughout your stay. Please note that diving and pool swimming should not be combined immediately — follow standard post-dive surface interval guidelines before entering the pool.

How do I choose the right accommodation for my trip? +

If you are primarily focused on diving and prefer to keep costs low, the Basic or Standard options offer everything you need for a comfortable stay. If you would like more space, added services, or dining options on-site, the Premium or Deluxe options are the natural choice. When you build your dive holiday, you can compare options side by side.
