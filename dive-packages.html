Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our dive packages

What experience level do I need for these packages? +

Most of our packages require a minimum PADI Open Water (or equivalent) certification. The Tiger Shark Escape and Ultimate Fuvahmulah Experience are suitable for all certified divers. The Pelagic Paradise Week is best suited to Advanced Open Water divers or those with 20+ logged dives, as some sites reach 30–40m. Not yet certified? We can arrange your PADI course — just ask!

Are tiger shark encounters guaranteed? +

Tiger sharks are resident year-round in Fuvahmulah and are encountered on the vast majority of dives at our key pelagic sites. While no wildlife encounter can ever be 100% guaranteed, our local guides know exactly where and when to find them. Our encounter rate is extremely high — most guests see tiger sharks on their very first dive.

What's the best time of year to visit? +

Fuvahmulah is diveable year-round. October to May offers the calmest seas and best visibility. June to September brings warmer waters and frequent manta ray and thresher shark sightings. Tiger sharks are present in every month. We help you choose the best dates based on your target species and budget.

How do I get to Fuvahmulah? +

Fly internationally to Malé (MLE), then take a Maldivian Airlines or FlyMe domestic flight to Fuvahmulah (FVM) — around 50 minutes. We'll guide you on booking the best connections and will handle all transfers once you land in Fuvahmulah. Note: the domestic flight is not included in packages but we make arranging it easy.

What dive equipment is provided? +

All packages include full dive equipment rental: BCD, regulator, wetsuit (3mm), mask, fins, tanks, and weights. If you have your own equipment, you're welcome to bring it — just let us know and we'll adjust accordingly. Underwater camera equipment and dive torches are available as add-ons.

How many divers are in each group? +

We keep groups small — a maximum of 4 divers per guide. This ensures personalised attention, better safety underwater, and a more intimate wildlife encounter experience. Couples and solo travellers are all welcome. If you'd like a fully private guide, ask about our private boat charter add-on.

Can I customise a package? +

Absolutely — all our packages are fully customisable. Want more nights, extra dives, a private guide, or specific marine life targets? Just tell us what you're looking for and we'll build a tailored quote. Contact us on WhatsApp or email bookings@staywavemaldives.com.

What is the cancellation & refund policy? +

We understand that travel plans can change. We offer flexible rescheduling for bookings cancelled more than 30 days before arrival. Cancellations within 30 days may be subject to partial charges depending on supplier costs already incurred. Full details are provided with your quote. We strongly recommend travel insurance that covers trip cancellation.
