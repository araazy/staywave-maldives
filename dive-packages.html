Package FAQ

Common questions about booking your Fuvahmulah dive package

How do I book a package? +

The quickest way is to message us on WhatsApp at +960 797 2103. Tell us your preferred travel dates, group size, and experience level. We'll confirm availability, send a detailed quote, and hold your dates once a deposit is paid.

What's the minimum certification required? +

All participants must hold a valid PADI Open Water certification (or equivalent from NAUI, SSI, CMAS, or British Sub-Aqua Club). Advanced Open Water is recommended for dives at deeper sites such as Deepend Finolhu. If you're not yet certified, we can arrange a PADI course before your package begins.

Are the packages suitable for beginners? +

Yes — our Tiger Shark Escape package is excellent for newly certified divers. Most tiger shark encounters happen at 15–25 m, well within the Open Water limit. Our guides maintain maximum 4 divers per guide, ensuring close personal attention for less experienced divers.

Can I customise the number of dives? +

Absolutely. Our Custom Package is fully flexible. You can add or remove dives from any package at a per-dive rate. Some guests prefer 1 dive per day to have more time exploring the island; others opt for 3 dives per day to maximise their pelagic encounters.

What is the Marine Park fee and who pays it? +

The Maldives Marine Park conservation fee is USD 6 per dive, paid directly to the Maldives Marine Research Institute. This fee is not included in our packages but we collect it on behalf of the authority on the first dive day. It goes directly toward protecting Fuvahmulah's marine ecosystem.

How do I get to Fuvahmulah? +

Fly into Malé (MLE) then take a domestic Maldivian Air Taxi or FlyMe flight to GAN Airport (GAN) in Addu Atoll. From GAN, a second domestic flight or speedboat transfer reaches Fuvahmulah (FVM). Return airport transfers between GAN and Fuvahmulah are included in all packages. We can assist with booking your domestic legs on request.

What's the cancellation and refund policy? +

We require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking. The deposit is fully refundable if you cancel more than 60 days before arrival. For cancellations within 60–30 days, 50% of the deposit is retained. For cancellations within 30 days, the full deposit is non-refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance that covers trip cancellations.

Is solo travel/diving possible? +

Yes! Solo travellers are very welcome. We can pair you with other divers in our small groups, or arrange a private guide for a solo experience. Solo supplements are minimal — contact us for a personalised quote. Many of our guests travel alone and love the intimate atmosphere Fuvahmulah offers.
