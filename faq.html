Travel and Arrival Questions

Start with the practical points that shape your itinerary.

How do I get to Fuvahmulah?

Most guests travel internationally to Malé and then connect onward domestically to Fuvahmulah. Exact routing depends on your wider itinerary and flight schedule.

How many days should I plan?

That depends on your travel time, dive goals, and how much flexibility you want for weather or rest days. Many guests prefer a trip length that allows both quality diving and comfortable transfers.

Can you help me understand arrival timing?

Yes. Sharing your preferred dates and flight ideas early helps us explain what timing is most practical for accommodation, transfers, and first dive days.

Diving and Stay Questions

These answers cover the parts of the trip that matter most once you are on the island.

What is the diving like?

Fuvahmulah is known for exciting pelagic-focused diving and changing conditions. The experience is often best suited to divers who are comfortable in open water and happy to stay flexible.

Can I choose different accommodation standards?

Yes. The diving package stays consistent while the accommodation category can be adjusted to suit your comfort level and budget.

Do marine life encounters happen on every dive?

No. Fuvahmulah has an exceptional reputation, but wildlife sightings can never be guaranteed and plans always depend on real conditions.

Planning and Booking Questions

Useful guidance for preparing your enquiry and next steps.

What information should I send first?

Dates, number of travellers, dive certification level, recent experience, and preferred accommodation style are all helpful starting points.

Do you provide booking functionality on the website?

No. The website is for information and planning only, and trip requests are handled through direct communication.

Should I arrange travel insurance?

Yes. Insurance that covers scuba diving, trip interruption, and relevant medical support is strongly recommended for any diving holiday.

What if I have special requests?

Let us know about meal needs, room preferences, group travel, or anything that may influence your ideal itinerary so we can plan around it.
