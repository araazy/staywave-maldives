How These Terms Work

These terms explain the basis on which StayWave Maldives provides information, trip planning support, and holiday coordination services.

All quotations, recommendations, and itinerary guidance are provided subject to availability, operational conditions, and confirmation by the relevant accommodation, transport, and dive partners. Nothing on the website creates a guaranteed reservation or service commitment on its own.

By proceeding with trip planning or accepting a confirmed arrangement, guests are expected to review these terms carefully and make sure the proposed holiday matches their own travel and diving requirements.

  • Operational realitySchedules, partner availability, and local conditions may require reasonable adjustments.
  • Guest responsibilityAccurate travel, health, and diving information is important for safe and suitable trip planning.
