Understanding Cookies on This Website

Cookies are small data files that help websites remember settings, support performance, and understand how visitors use key pages.

StayWave Maldives aims to use site technologies in a measured and practical way. Cookies may help basic functionality, improve the browsing experience, and provide broad insight into how visitors interact with the website over time.

Because website tools and hosting arrangements can evolve, this policy is intended to explain the main categories of cookies and the choices available to visitors in clear, general terms.

  • Essential supportSome cookies help core site functions work as expected.
  • Experience improvementsSome cookies can remember settings or preferences between visits.
  • Usage insightAnalytics-style cookies may help us understand which pages are most useful to visitors.

Types of Cookies We May Use

The mix of cookies may vary depending on the tools and services active on the site.

Essential Cookies

These support core technical functions such as navigation, basic security, and maintaining a working browsing session.

Preference Cookies

These may remember simple settings or choices that make repeat visits feel more consistent and convenient.

Analytics Cookies

These may provide high-level information about visits, page popularity, or user flow so we can understand how the website is performing.

How Cookies Help the Website

Cookies are used to support the site rather than to create booking flows or personal profiling features here.

Performance Monitoring

We may review broad analytics data to understand which content visitors use most and where the site experience can be improved.

Basic Site Behaviour

Some scripts or technical tools may rely on temporary storage or cookie-like behaviour to function correctly across pages.

Consistency Across Visits

Preference-related tools may help the site remember a setting or present content more consistently on future visits.

Third Parties and Your Choices

Visitors should understand both the role of external services and the controls available to them.

Third-Party Services

Some cookies may be set by trusted third-party services used for analytics, embedded functionality, or website support.

Browser Controls

Most browsers let you review, block, or delete cookies through their privacy settings, although some site functions may behave differently afterwards.

Device Preferences

You can also use device or browser-level privacy features to limit tracking technologies or clear stored data between visits.

Policy Updates

If the website’s tooling changes in a meaningful way, this policy may be updated to reflect how cookies are being used at that time.

Practical Notes for Visitors

A few final reminders can help you decide how to manage cookies on your own device.

Review Settings Regularly

Privacy controls differ by browser, so it is worth checking the options available in the specific browser you use most.

Shared Devices

If you browse on a shared device, clearing cookies and saved data may help protect your privacy between users.

Analytics Are Aggregated

Where analytics tools are used, the goal is typically to understand general site behaviour rather than to create a booking profile on this website.

Questions Welcome

If you need clarification about website technologies, contact StayWave Maldives and we can explain the policy in practical terms.
