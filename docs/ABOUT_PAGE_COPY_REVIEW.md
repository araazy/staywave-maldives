## Summary of Wording Changes

The About page has been rewritten to consistently position StayWave Maldives as a **premium Maldives travel agency and personalised holiday planner**, rather than a dive operator. All language that implied StayWave Maldives employs dive guides, operates dive centres, or provides dive instruction has been removed or replaced with travel agency and planning language.

## SEO Improvements

### Keyword Strategy Shift

**Before:** The page targeted keywords around diving, dive holidays, and Fuvahmulah dive trips — positioning the business as a dive operator.

**After:** The page now targets keywords aligned with the travel agency and holiday planning market:

- `Maldives travel agency`
- `personalised Maldives holiday planner`
- `Maldives holiday planning`
- `trusted Maldives travel partner`
- `Fuvahmulah travel experts`

### Title Tag

The updated title (`About StayWave Maldives | Maldives Travel Agency & Holiday Planner`) directly targets high-intent search queries from travellers seeking a Maldives travel agency.

### Meta Description

Updated to include actionable language and the agency positioning: "trusted Maldives travel agency and personalised holiday planner" — which improves click-through relevance for non-diving travel searches.

### Structured Data (JSON-LD)

The `mainEntity` schema description now reflects the travel agency role and includes references to carefully selected accommodation, trusted dive centre partners, and guest support — aligning schema with the page content and improving rich snippet potential.

### Brand Positioning Consistency

The brand positioning statement has been woven into the hero subtitle, ensuring early on-page keyword reinforcement:
> "StayWave Maldives is your trusted Maldives travel partner, helping you plan personalised island holidays through carefully selected accommodation, dive centres and unforgettable local experiences."

## Future Content Recommendations

### 1. Add a Mission & Vision section
The About page currently does not have explicitly labelled Mission and Vision statements. Adding a dedicated section would improve brand clarity and provide additional SEO-rich content opportunities:
- **Mission:** "To make personalised Maldives holiday planning accessible, transparent, and genuinely memorable for every guest."
- **Vision:** "To be the most trusted travel partner for independent travellers discovering the Maldives."

### 2. Add a Statistics / Social Proof section
Adding a statistics row (e.g. guests hosted, years operating, islands covered, average review rating) would increase trust signals and SEO content depth.

### 3. Expand the Team section with real photography
Replacing the current placeholder images with genuine team photography will significantly improve engagement and build trust with prospective guests.

### 4. Add guest testimonials to the About page
A curated testimonials section on the About page would reinforce the travel agency positioning and provide fresh, indexable content. This could pull from a reviews section or use structured data (`Review` schema) for SEO benefit.

### 5. Create a dedicated Partnerships page
As the brand positioning emphasises trusted local partnerships (guesthouses, dive centres, transfer providers), a dedicated Partners or Trusted Partners page would:
- Provide a natural link target from the About page
- Improve E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) signals
- Create additional SEO content around partner names and destinations

### 6. Consider renaming "Build My Dive Holiday" CTA
The primary call-to-action across the site still uses "Build My Dive Holiday" — which conflicts with the travel agency positioning. Consider testing a broader label such as **"Plan My Maldives Holiday"** or **"Start Planning"** to appeal to non-diving guests as well.
