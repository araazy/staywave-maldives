Marine Life of Fuvahmulah

Dive with the world's most incredible pelagic species.

Tiger Shark

Tiger sharks are Fuvahmulah's signature encounter, known for consistent sightings in clear blue water and unforgettable close-range moments with experienced local guides.

Thresher Shark

Thresher sharks appear in deeper blue-water conditions, rewarding patient divers with one of the island's most distinctive pelagic sightings.

Hammerhead Shark

Schooling hammerheads are a prized sight in the right conditions, adding a dramatic early-morning pelagic highlight to the destination.

Whale Shark

Whale sharks pass through Fuvahmulah waters seasonally, offering lucky guests the chance to encounter the ocean's largest fish during wider pelagic excursions.

Oceanic Manta Ray

Oceanic manta rays bring graceful movement to the blue, complementing the island's shark encounters with another truly memorable big-animal experience.
