Underwater Photography Tips

1. Camera Setup

  • Full-frame or crop sensor cameras
  • Action cameras for beginners
  • Ensure proper maintenance after each dive

2. Recommended Lenses

  • Wide-angle lens for sharks and manta rays
  • Fisheye lens for close encounters
  • Dome port for split-level photography

3. Best Practices

  • Maintain neutral buoyancy
  • Avoid chasing marine life
  • Respect wildlife behavior
  • Keep a safe distance

4. Camera Care

  • Rinse equipment with fresh water
  • Check O-rings before every dive
  • Dry equipment thoroughly before storage
