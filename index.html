Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about diving in Maldives

Is tiger shark diving safe? +

Absolutely! Tiger sharks are curious but not aggressive toward humans. Our experienced guides follow strict safety protocols. We maintain a safe distance and use proven techniques developed over decades of diving in Fuvahmulah.

What's the best time to visit? +

Fuvahmulah is incredible year-round! October-May offers the best weather and calmest seas. June-September brings warmer waters and more pelagic action. We dive every single day of the year.

Do I need to be certified? +

For most dives, yes. We require Open Water certification or equivalent. If you're not certified, we offer PADI certification courses in Fuvahmulah or can arrange training before arrival.

What's included in the packages? +

Packages typically include dives, accommodation, airport transfers, and most meals. The exact inclusions depend on your chosen package. We're happy to customize based on your budget and preferences.

How many divers per guide? +

We keep group sizes small—maximum 4 divers per guide. This ensures personalized attention, safety, and the best possible diving experience for everyone.

Can you arrange accommodations? +

Yes! We partner with premium resorts and guesthouses across Maldives. From budget-friendly options to ultra-luxury, we'll find the perfect place for your stay and budget.
