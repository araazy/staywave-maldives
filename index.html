Marine Life Encounters

Discover the incredible biodiversity of Maldivian waters

Manta Rays

Manta birostris

Gentle giants of the ocean. Spot these graceful creatures gliding through the water during seasonal migrations (June-November).

Sea Turtles

Chelonoidis spp.

Ancient mariners of the reef. Green and hawksbill turtles are commonly seen around our dive sites throughout the year.

Reef Sharks

Various species

Magnificent predators. Whitetip and blacktip reef sharks patrol the reefs, creating thrilling yet safe diving experiences.

Clownfish

Amphiprion ocellaris

Iconic reef inhabitants. Explore anemone gardens and observe these colorful fish in their natural habitat.

Spinner Dolphins

Stenella longirostris

Playful marine mammals. Pod encounters are common during early morning boat rides to dive sites.

Eagle Rays

Aetobatus narinari

Elegant swimmers. These spotted rays are often spotted cruising the sandy bottoms of our prime dive locations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your dive

Do I need to be certified to dive? +

No! Our Discover Dive package is designed for beginners. You'll receive PADI certification during your first two dives. For advanced dives, current certification is required.

What is the best time to visit for diving? +

Year-round diving is possible in the Maldives! The dry season (November-April) offers the best visibility and calmest seas. June-November is prime season for manta ray encounters.

What is the maximum depth we dive to? +

Beginner dives go to 12m, intermediate to 30m, and advanced dives to 40m+. All depths follow international diving safety standards based on certification level.

Is diving safe? What safety measures are in place? +

Safety is our top priority. All equipment is regularly maintained and inspected. Our instructors are PADI-certified with years of experience. We follow strict safety protocols and maintain small group sizes (max 4 per guide).

What should I bring for diving? +

We provide all diving equipment (tank, BCD, wetsuit, fins). Bring swimwear, sunscreen, underwater camera (optional), and any personal medications. We'll provide a detailed packing list upon booking.

Can I get a refund if I cancel? +

Cancellations made 30 days before your trip receive a full refund. Cancellations 15-30 days prior receive 50% refund. Cancellations within 15 days are non-refundable but can be rescheduled.
